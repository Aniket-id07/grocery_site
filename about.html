History


FreshGro began with a simple dream – to make fresh, chemical-free, and organic food accessible to every household. What started as a small initiative by a group of passionate food lovers and farmers quickly grew into a trusted brand. Over the years, we built strong relationships with local farming communities, supporting them in adopting natural farming practices while bringing consumers healthier, fresher, and safer products. Today, FreshGro is proud to be a bridge between farmers and families who value purity and sustainability.
